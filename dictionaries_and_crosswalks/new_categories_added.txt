New Categories Added:
The "Vague Pain" Cocktail (Part D):

Muscle Relaxants: Cyclobenzaprine, Carisoprodol (Soma), Methocarbamol. High discretion, often low value.

Gabapentinoids: Gabapentin/Lyrica. The new "safe" pain pill that is widely overprescribed for non-specific pain.

Oral Steroids: Methylprednisolone (Medrol Dosepak). The ultimate "I want to feel better for my wedding this weekend" drug.

The "Viral Fix" (Part D):

Cough Syrups: Promethazine w/ Codeine. Highly sought after, clinically dubious for simple colds.

The "Sleep Fix" (Part D):

Z-Drugs: Zolpidem (Ambien), Eszopiclone (Lunesta). "I can't sleep" is a classic satisfaction driver.

The "High-Margin" Clinic (Part B):

Ophthalmology: Lucentis/Eylea (Macular Degeneration). Massive cost difference vs. Avastin (the cheap alternative).

Bone Density: Prolia/Xgeva. High-cost injections.

The "Fishing Expedition" (Services):

Cardiac Stress Testing: Nuclear stress tests (78452). A cash cow for cardiology.

CT Scans: Head CT for headache, Sinus CT for "congestion."

Echo: Transthoracic Echocardiogram (93306)
